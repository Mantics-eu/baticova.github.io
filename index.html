About Baticova

Baticova builds dependable autonomous AI workforces — digital departments that plan, coordinate, and deliver real work under human control. Our mission is to make AI collaboration transparent, auditable, and human-governed.

From Prompts to Projects

The Problem

AI today is powerful but static — a brilliant employee that still needs a manager for each step. It generates output, but it doesn’t coordinate work.

The Baticova Approach

Baticova provides the infrastructure for dependable autonomy: digital departments that can plan, coordinate, and execute complex projects with clear oversight.

Governed Autonomy You Can Trust

Transparency

Every decision is recorded in an auditable log. Inspect plans, tasks, evidence, and results at any time.

Memory & Context

Shared memory gives agents durable context about your business, so work improves with every run.

Human Control

Guardrails and approvals keep you in charge. Autonomy where it helps; intervention where it matters.

The AI Workforce

Orchestrators plan, specialists execute, reviewers validate — all visible in shared memory.

Orchestrator

Turns goals into plans, assigns work, tracks dependencies, and escalates for approval.

Specialists

Research, write, build, test, and deploy — domain-specific agents for real work products.

Reviewer

Checks quality, verifies evidence, and enforces standards before anything ships.

Swarm AI — Coordinated Teams, Dependable Outcomes

Collective Intelligence

Baticova deploys a coordinated swarm of specialist agents that collaborate through explicit plans and shared state.

swarm_architect

A meta-agent monitors results, tunes workflows, and evolves the swarm within your guardrails — improving reliability over time.

Architecture (Local-First, Cloud-Ready)

Brains

Open models via Ollama locally, or securely in the cloud — powering orchestrator and specialist agents.

Memory & Log

PostgreSQL as shared memory, job queue, and flight recorder for full auditability.

Execution Engine

Workflows with n8n / Node-RED plan & run tasks, call models/tools, and update state.

Observability

Grafana dashboards for visibility, metrics, and human oversight.
